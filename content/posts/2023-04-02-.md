### 1. What is the range of 3GPP defined FR1 & FR2?

The current range if FR1 & FR2 defined by 3GPP is:

  * FR1: 410 MHz – 7.125 GHz
  * FR2: 24.25 GHz – 52.6 GHz

### 2. How can you decide the use of spectrum based on its operating frequency?

The operating frequency can be divided further into following layers:

  * Coverage Layer &#8211; for Sub-1 GHz frequencies
  * Capacity Layer &#8211; for 1-6 GHz frequencies
  * Ultra low latency layers &#8211; for 24.25 – 52.6 GHz range
