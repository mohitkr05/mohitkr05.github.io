Wired is a monthly American magazine, published in print and online editions. The AI category of the Wired Magazine focuses on the impact of AI on governance and society.

&nbsp;

#### [MIT Technology Review][2]

<img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="" src="https://wp.technologyreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/MIT_7Topics_final_AI.png" width="415" height="415" /> 

MIT Technology Review is a **magazine wholly owned by the Massachusetts Institute of Technology**, but editorially independent of the university. It is one of the very advanced technology review website available on the internet.

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

### **[IEEE Spectrum][3]**

IEEE Spectrum is a **magazine edited by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers**. The IEEE&#8217;s description of it is: IEEE Spectrum is the flagship magazine and website of the IEEE, the world’s largest professional organization devoted to engineering and the applied sciences.

&nbsp;

#### **[AI Trends][4]**

AI Trends is the leading industry media channel focused on the business and technology of AI. It is designed to keep executives ahead of the curve.

#### **[Science Daily][5]**

Science Daily is an **American website** that aggregates press releases and publishes lightly edited press releases.

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

### **[Extreme Tech][6]**

ExtremeTech is a technology weblog about hardware, computer software, science and other technologies
